# AI-Powered ATS for Faster Hiring

## Headline
Hire the Right Candidates Faster with AI

## Subheadline
Streamline your recruitment process, reduce manual work, and focus on hiring top talent.

---

## Problem
Hiring is slow and inefficient. Recruiters spend too much time reviewing CVs, managing candidates manually, and coordinating communication.

---

## Solution
An AI-powered Applicant Tracking System (ATS) that helps you:
- Centralize all candidates in one place  
- Automate initial screening  
- Improve hiring decisions with structured data  

---

## Benefits
- Reduce time-to-hire  
- Improve candidate quality  
- Save recruiter time  
- Make data-driven hiring decisions  

---

## How It Works
1. Upload candidates into the system  
2. AI screens and ranks applicants  
3. Review top candidates quickly  
4. Move faster from application to hire
